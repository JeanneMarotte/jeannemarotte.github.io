Gothic Lolita

A project I made with a friend, Solenn Lelaisant. She designed the sets and was in charge of the modelling . I drew the character design and did the texturing, shading and rendering parts.

We wanted to have a cute modelling with a render a bit more realistic/ PBR than what we usually do. We drew inspiration from the gothic lolita alternative fashion.
